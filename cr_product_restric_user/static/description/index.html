Overview

Product Restrict Users Odoo App is designed to provide greater control over product visibility within the Odoo system. This app allows administrators to restrict access to specific products or product categories based on the roles and permissions of individual users. By assigning particular products or product categories to users, businesses can streamline their sales processes, limit access to sensitive items, and prevent unauthorized sales.

Product Restrict Users

  • Go to Settings >> User & Companies> Users , and enable the "Product Restriction on Users".
  • Set "Product" as the "Restriction On" option and choose the products you want to grant access to for a particular user.
  • Only the products that are allowed for the user will be visible and accessible in all models.
  • The user is allowed to select only the authorized products in any model.
  • Set "Category" as the "Restriction On" option and choose the product categories you want to grant access to for a particular user.
  • Only the product categories that are allowed for the user will be visible and accessible in all models.
  • Only the products with allowed product categories will be visible and accessible in all models.
